# Binary-2048 Merch And Swag Strategy

## Goal

Create a lightweight physical merch track that supports the brand and community without entangling physical goods with the in-game store economy.

This merch layer should:

- reinforce the Binary-2048 identity
- give players and bot-builders something fun to wear or collect
- stay operationally simple
- avoid turning the app team into a shipping-heavy retail business too early

## Scope

Initial merch should be limited to low-complexity physical goods:

- hats
- shirts
- stickers

Possible secondary items later:

- mugs
- desk mats
- posters

## Separation From Game Economy

Physical merch must remain separate from the in-game store.

Rules:

- merch purchases do not grant gameplay power
- merch does not unlock ranked advantages
- merch can optionally unlock cosmetic recognition only if clearly labeled and non-competitive
- merch inventory, fulfillment, and refunds should not share logic with tile/store consumables

This keeps the player economy understandable and avoids pay-to-win confusion.

## Brand Direction

Primary brand:

- **Binary 2048**

Secondary slogan/acronym explorations:

- **FBBB**
- **For Bots By Bots**
- **F-cubed**

The merch line should feel adjacent to the core product rather than replacing it. The main Binary-2048 mark should remain the anchor identity.

## Recommended Launch Shape

Start with a tiny merch capsule:

1. one logo tee
2. one hat
3. one sticker pack

This keeps:

- art scope small
- QA simple
- inventory and supplier risk low

## Storefront Recommendation

Use an external storefront or print-on-demand surface first.

Recommendation order:

1. external print-on-demand storefront
2. simple branded landing page that links out
3. only later consider deeper in-app merch browsing

Reason:

- physical fulfillment and support should stay outside the game app early on
- external storefronts are better for shipping, taxes, and order support

## Success Criteria

The first merch launch should optimize for:

- brand fit
- low operational drag
- community interest
- acceptable margin after fees and fulfillment

It should not optimize for:

- large SKU count
- custom warehousing
- complicated bundles

## Recommendation Summary

- Start with a small external merch offering
- Keep merch separate from in-game economy and store logic
- Lead with hats, shirts, and stickers
- Keep Binary-2048 as the main brand, with `FBBB` / `For Bots By Bots` as a supporting line to validate
