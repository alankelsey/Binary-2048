# Player Monetization Policy

Binary-2048 is designed to stay fair and transparent.

## What can be paid

- Cosmetic items (themes, visual packs).
- Convenience/QoL items in non-ranked contexts (for example, extra undo charges where allowed by mode).
- Optional subscription for ad-free and feature access.

## What is cosmetic

- Theme packs and visual styles.
- UI-only presentation changes.
- Cosmetic effects that do not change score outcomes.

## What is never pay-to-win

- Ranked leaderboard outcomes.
- `ranked_pure` session eligibility.
- Core deterministic replay validation and anti-cheat controls.

## Ads policy

- If ads are enabled, they are rewarded and opt-in only.
- Paid tier remains ad-free.
- Ad rewards cannot make a run `ranked_pure` eligible.

## Integrity commitment

- Server-authoritative checks enforce ranked integrity.
- Boosted/assisted runs are separated from pure ranked outcomes.
- We will document policy changes before enabling new monetization mechanics.
