# Binary-2048 Merch Concept Pack

## Purpose

Define the first round of low-risk merch concepts so we can review tone, brand fit, and product mix before spending time on polished production art.

This concept pack is intentionally lightweight:

- product direction
- copy direction
- logo placement ideas
- color and print constraints

Not included yet:

- production-ready vector files
- supplier-specific print templates
- final costed mockups

## Core Visual Rules

- Keep the primary Binary-2048 wordmark as the anchor mark
- Use `FBBB` / `For Bots By Bots` as a supporting line, not the sole identity
- Avoid cluttered game-board layouts that become unreadable on fabric
- Favor 1-color or 2-color print approaches for the first run

## Concept 1: Classic Logo Tee

Product:

- soft black or midnight navy shirt

Front:

- Binary-2048 wordmark across chest
- small 2x2 tile icon lockup near the left side or centered above subtitle

Back:

- optional small line near neck:
  - `For Bots By Bots`

Why it works:

- safe first SKU
- clearly tied to the app
- low print risk

## Concept 2: FBBB Bot Builder Tee

Product:

- dark charcoal or slate tee

Front:

- `FBBB` acronym in bold block treatment
- subtext:
  - `For Bots By Bots`

Back:

- small Binary-2048 badge or tile mark

Why it works:

- more insider/dev-community flavor
- good for players who like the bot-first identity
- still readable without knowing the whole brand story

## Concept 3: Minimal Hat

Product:

- black or navy cap

Front options:

1. small Binary-2048 tile icon
2. embroidered `FBBB`

Back:

- tiny `binary2048.com` or no back copy

Why it works:

- embroidery-friendly
- clean and wearable
- low complexity

## Concept 4: Sticker Pack

Contents:

- main Binary-2048 logo sticker
- tile icon sticker
- `FBBB` text sticker
- one playful slogan sticker:
  - `For Bots By Bots`

Why it works:

- cheap entry item
- easy to bundle
- good for laptops and dev setups

## Color Direction

Keep the early merch palette close to the app:

- midnight navy
- black
- off-white
- electric blue accent
- optional teal accent for specialty variants

Avoid:

- too many gradients in first-print runs
- thin detail that will not survive embroidery or small stickers

## Copy Direction

Preferred lines:

- `Binary 2048`
- `For Bots By Bots`
- `FBBB`

Optional line to test later:

- `Made mostly for bots by mostly bots`

That longer phrase is better as a limited-run or back-print concept than a primary front mark.

## First Review Recommendation

Start review with these three SKUs:

1. classic logo tee
2. `FBBB` tee
3. embroidered hat

Stickers should be the low-risk add-on if those concepts feel right.

## Decision Gate

Before moving to production art, confirm:

- the main brand stays stronger than the merch joke line
- `FBBB` reads as clever rather than confusing
- each concept still looks good in one-color print
- there is at least one non-insider design for broader appeal
